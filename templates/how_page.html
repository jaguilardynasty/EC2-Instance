How it works

Our software uses a combination of advanced Machine Learning algorithms to optimize for the best possible results. These models are trained on thousands of Privacy Policies and Terms agreements, across a huge range of industries.

Our algorithms are programmed to find the most alarming sentences for the everyday person, not those that are obvious, redundant, or unimportant.

Due to the probabilistic nature of machine learning, we can't promise that every result will be perfect, however it will be the clauses with the highest probability of being important that are returned to you.
